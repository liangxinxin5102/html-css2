= GreenPage =

GreenPage is an elegant, minimalist blogging theme. It has been designed for people who create a variety of types of content. The theme displays excerpts, custom menus  and thumbnail images on the your blog.

Slider shows sticky posts.
Mark your answer several posts as sticky.
Under side menu of post/ "Publish" / "Visibility". Click "Edit"/ Check Box "Stick this post to the front page"
